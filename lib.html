My Personal Library     致知草堂 Bibliotheca Scientiam

Once awhile, we, book lovers, may have either a crave or just a simple need for a book. Sometimes, I found that a book can be extremely costly to possess and difficult to obtain through other means. Therefore, I would love to share them to those who need them. I, as a physics student, have collected a few classic textbooks, references, fun reads, etc., which I would love to share as long as they are not currently read by me. So I built this site to circulate the books I have for readers like me. I am excited to meet more book lovers and fellow physicists.

The first time you borrow a book from me, I will provide you a patron account which you can track your requests online. Then, you can check books out by contacting me directly or by reserving one on the website and picking it up from my place. Do be mindful that these are my private collections, and some of my collection contain a great deal of memories. I hope these memories pass down to you while you treat and care them as if they were your own collection.

Enjoy! -- Y.B.
